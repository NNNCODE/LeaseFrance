# Disclaimer

Baillio is an assistance tool for private landlords. It helps organize rental
data and produce working documents. It does not provide legal, tax, or
accounting advice, and using it does not create any advisor relationship.

## No Legal, Tax, Or Accounting Advice

- App content, labels, reminders, and generated documents are provided for
  convenience only.
- French rental, energy, and tax rules change over time and depend on each
  situation. The app may lag behind current law.
- For any decision with legal or financial consequences, consult a qualified
  professional (lawyer, notaire, accountant, or tax advisor).

## Documents And Calculations To Verify Yourself

Users remain responsible for checking that each output fits their case before
relying on it or sending it to a third party. In particular:

- `quittance` and `recu` documents: verify amounts, periods, parties, and
  whether a full or partial receipt is the correct document for the payment.
- IRL rent revision: verify the IRL reference values against the current INSEE
  publication and confirm the revision clause and dates in the lease allow it.
- DPE indicators: energy-performance badges and rental-restriction dates in the
  app are informational. Verify the applicable rules and dates for the property
  before signing, renewing, or advertising a lease.
- Fiscal export: the CSV and PDF summaries are working documents, not a tax
  return and not an automatic filing. Verify figures against your own records
  and the tax rules that apply to you.

## PDF Templates Are Not A Substitute For Professional Review

Generated PDF documents (receipts, lease contracts, revision notices, deposit
documents, inspection reports, reminder letters, fiscal summaries) are based on
common templates. They are not guaranteed to be complete, current, or valid for
every situation, and they do not replace review by a qualified professional.
Read every generated document before signing or sending it.

## Personal Data Responsibility

Users act as the data controller for the tenant and guarantor personal data
they enter into the app. Users are responsible for:

- collecting only the data they are allowed to collect
- informing the persons concerned where required
- storing the data securely, including backups
- deleting data when there is no longer a valid reason to keep it

Baillio stores data locally on the user's machine but does not manage these
obligations for the user. See [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md) for where data lives.
